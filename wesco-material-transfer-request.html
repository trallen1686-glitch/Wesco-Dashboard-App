Wesco Material Transfer Request

This form is used by Project Managers to request inventory or material to be moved from one project to another, or from a job back into inventory. This helps protect job cost accuracy, invoice tracking, financial reporting, and material integrity.

PMs should use the job named Inventory when locating available material or inventory related invoice information. The request should clearly identify the invoice, vendor, item, quantity, cost, and the job receiving or releasing the material.
